Fat Suit for "Falstaff"

Merry Wives Of Windsor

Florence-Carlton Shakespeare Company 2023
Design: Hannah Brooklynn McLean
Larger, fake body was built upon from a T-shirt that fit the actress' body. The fat suit body was built out using batting and stuffing that was hand-sewn on to the base T-shirt, then covered and hand-sewn in a final layer of white spandex fabric.
