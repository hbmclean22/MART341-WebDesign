French Toast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Beat egg, vanilla, and cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on a lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides.
